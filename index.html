One family-owned company, two locations, same superb service. The Ethan's Coffee Heaven cafes, with locations in Labasa and Savusavu, are well-known for serving the best coffee in town.

Ethan's Coffee Heaven offers a good selection of traditional Fijian, Indian, and foreign foods in addition to its excellent coffee. It is popular with locals and visitors for the great menu and friendly service.
